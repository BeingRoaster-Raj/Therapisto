About Therapisto

Therapisto was founded with the mission to bring holistic healing to the community. Our clinic offers a diverse range of therapeutic treatments, combining evidence-based practices and compassionate care. Our team of certified therapists continually updates their skills to incorporate the latest advances in therapy and wellness.

We believe in a personalized approach that treats the individual, not just the symptoms. Each treatment plan is designed after thorough evaluation to ensure the best outcomes. Whether you seek relief from chronic pain, recovery from injury, or stress management, Therapisto provides a supportive environment for your healing.

Our facilities are equipped with modern equipment, and our services include vacuum & fire cupping, chiropractic treatment, dry needling, acupressure, auricular therapy, pain management, and much more. We are committed to helping you regain vitality and improve your quality of life.
